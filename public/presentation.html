Challenge
The Problem
⏱️

30% Time Wasted on Paperwork

Doctors manually sort through messy unstructured health records — handwritten prescriptions, scanned pathology PDFs, and legacy hospital summaries.

💸

Expensive & Complex Search Systems

Traditional clinical search engines are slow, require complex database setups, and cost hospitals thousands in licensing fees.

🚨

HIPAA Compliance Violations

Sensitive Patient Health Information (PHI) is routinely sent to public cloud APIs, violating HIPAA regulations and risking data breaches.

Solution
Our Solution
📄

Open Knowledge Format (OKF)

Converts raw medical records (PDF, DOCX, TXT, Images) into structured, human-readable, version-controlled Markdown with YAML frontmatter.

⚡

1-LLM-Call Optimization

Our local query planner resolves clinical Q&A in maximum 1 LLM call — reducing token costs by 75% and latency by 80%.

🛡️

On-Premise Privacy Shield

Local Ollama LLM deployment ensures patient data never leaves the hospital firewall. Full HIPAA compliance out of the box.
